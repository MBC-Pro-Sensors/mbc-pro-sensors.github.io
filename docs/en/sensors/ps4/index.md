## 🚀 Product Overview

Traditional dual-hub Bluetooth solutions are resource-heavy and prone to dropping. Infrared remotes lack buttons and have directional limits. 2.4G solutions are easily interfered with in multi-device environments.

The Controller Bluetooth Pro Receiver fundamentally solves these problems. Utilizing an **advanced Bluetooth communication protocol**, it maintains a stable connection even when dozens of equipment sets are operating simultaneously in the same venue. The **pairing memory feature** connects instantly upon power-on. A **tested 100-meter connection distance** ensures you are in control across any competition arena.

## 🎯 Core Advantages

- 📶 **Advanced Bluetooth Stability**: Multiple sets used simultaneously will not interfere with each other.
- 🧠 **Pairing Memory**: Pair once, auto-connect on power-on. Zero setup time before a match.
- 📏 **Tested 100m Distance**: Every corner of the competition arena remains under your control.
- 🎮 **Rich Button Layout**: Dual joysticks + analog triggers + directional pad + multi-function buttons.
- 🔌 **Plug & Play**: Plug directly into SPIKE / EV3; official blocks read data instantly.

## 🔌 Hardware Wiring & Pairing

1. Plug the receiver into SPIKE (Ports A~F) or EV3 (Ports 1~4).
2. **First-time Pairing**: Press the pairing button on the receiver, then turn on the controller and wait for the indicator light to remain steady.
3. **Subsequent Use**: Simply turn on the controller to automatically connect to the paired receiver.
