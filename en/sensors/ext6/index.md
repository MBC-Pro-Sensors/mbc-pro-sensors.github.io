> **Break the limits of hub ports: The 6-way expansion artifact with built-in isolated power protection, designed exclusively for advanced Python projects.**

## 🚀 Product Overview

The SPIKE Prime hub only has 6 connection ports, but large robotics projects often require multiple sensors and motors simultaneously, leaving you short on ports.

The SPIKE 6-Way Multiplexer lets you connect up to 6 sensors or motors using just a single port on your hub. Most importantly, it features an **independent power supply system**. High currents required by motors are not drawn from the main hub, **completely eliminating hub crashes caused by motor power consumption**. Additionally, the built-in **isolated protection circuit** ensures that any wiring issues will never damage your expensive SPIKE hub.

## 🎯 Core Advantages

- 🔌 **Break Port Limitations**: Expand 1 hub port into 6 usable interfaces.
- ⚡ **Independent Isolated Power**: Motor current is supplied independently. Draws no power from the hub, eliminating crash risks entirely.
- 🛡️ **Isolated Protection Circuit**: Electrical isolation design protects the SPIKE hub from abnormal current damage.
- 🐍 **Deep Python Integration**: Supports Pybricks Python environment, offering comprehensive multiplexer control capabilities.

## 🔌 Hardware Wiring

1. Plug the multiplexer into any port on the SPIKE hub.
2. Connect the independent power supply (battery box or adapter).
3. Connect your sensors and motors to the 6 expansion ports on the multiplexer.
4. Use corresponding commands in the Python environment to read data from each port.
